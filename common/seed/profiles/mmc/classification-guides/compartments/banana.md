# Banana Compartment (ðŸŒ) - Financial & Legal

## Overview

The Banana compartment protects **financial and legal** information including budgets, legal proceedings, regulatory compliance matters, and privileged communications.

## Scope

This compartment covers:
- Financial forecasts and projections
- Legal proceedings and litigation
- Regulatory filings and audits
- Contract negotiations
- M&A due diligence (pre-announcement)
- Insurance claims and settlements

## Access Requirements

> [!WARNING]
> Banana compartment access creates legal obligations. Misuse may constitute regulatory violations.

### Eligibility Criteria

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Department | Finance, Legal, Compliance, Executive |
| Certification | SOX compliance training (annual) |
| Approval | CFO/CLO or authorized delegate |
| Agreement | Signed confidentiality acknowledgment |

### Access by Role

| Role | Default Access |
|------|----------------|
| CFO/CLO | Full compartment access |
| Controllers | Financial data only |
| Legal Counsel | Legal matters + relevant financial |
| Compliance | Audit/regulatory subset |
| External Auditors | Read-only, time-limited |

## Attorney-Client Privilege

> [!CAUTION]
> Some materials in this compartment are protected by attorney-client privilege. Improper disclosure may waive this protection permanently.

### Protected Communications
- Direct communications with in-house counsel
- Legal advice memoranda
- Litigation strategy documents
- Settlement discussions

### Preserving Privilege
1. Mark documents "PRIVILEGED AND CONFIDENTIAL"
2. Limit distribution to need-to-know
3. Do not forward without Legal approval
4. Consult Legal before external disclosure

## Regulatory Considerations

### Material Non-Public Information (MNPI)
Financial data may constitute MNPI under securities laws:
- Do not trade company securities while in possession
- Do not share with anyone who might trade
- Report suspicious inquiries to Compliance

### Document Retention
Legal holds may apply:
- Check with Legal before deleting
- Preserve in original format
- Document chain of custody
