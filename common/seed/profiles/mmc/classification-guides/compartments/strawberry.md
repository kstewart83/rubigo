# Strawberry Compartment (ðŸ“) - Customer & Partner Data

## Overview

The Strawberry compartment protects **customer and partner confidential data** including customer PII, partner agreements, NDA-protected materials, and shared confidential information.

## Scope

This compartment covers:
- Customer personal information (PII)
- Customer business data
- Partner confidential information
- Joint venture materials
- NDA-protected documents
- Customer contracts and pricing
- Sales pipeline details

## Access Requirements

> [!WARNING]
> Customer data is subject to contractual obligations and privacy regulations. Breaches may result in legal liability and loss of customer trust.

### Eligibility Criteria

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Role | Customer-facing or partner-facing |
| Training | Data Protection Certification |
| Approval | Department head + Privacy Officer |
| Agreement | Customer Data Handling Acknowledgment |

### Need-to-Know Principle

Access is granted by customer/partner:
- Sales: Own accounts only
- Support: Active tickets only
- Success: Assigned customers only
- Partners: Specific partnership scope

## Customer PII Protection

### Categories

| Data Type | Sensitivity | Retention |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Contact Info | Standard | Active + 3 years |
| Payment Data | High (PCI) | Transaction + 7 years |
| Usage Data | Standard | Active + 1 year |
| Support Tickets | Standard | Resolution + 2 years |

## Partner Confidential Information

### NDA Obligations
- Review NDA terms before accessing
- Note any special handling requirements
- Track expiration dates
- Return/destroy per agreement

### Joint Development
- Segregate from internal R&D
- Maintain attribution records
- Follow IP allocation terms
- Separate access controls

## Data Subject Rights

Customers may request:
1. **Access**: Copy of their data
2. **Rectification**: Correct inaccuracies
3. **Erasure**: "Right to be forgotten"
4. **Portability**: Export in standard format
5. **Objection**: Opt-out of processing

Route requests to Privacy team within 24 hours.

## Breach Response

If customer data is potentially exposed:

1. **STOP** - Do not attempt remediation
2. **REPORT** - Notify Security immediately
3. **PRESERVE** - Maintain all evidence
4. **SUPPORT** - Assist investigation
5. **LEARN** - Participate in post-mortem

**Breach Hotline**: privacy@midwest-manufacturing.com
