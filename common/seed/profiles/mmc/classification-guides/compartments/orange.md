# Orange Compartment (ðŸŠ) - Human Resources

## Overview

The Orange compartment protects **human resources sensitive data** including performance reviews, disciplinary actions, compensation details, and employee personal information.

## Scope

This compartment covers:
- Personnel files and records
- Performance evaluations
- Disciplinary actions and investigations
- Compensation and benefits details
- Recruitment and hiring decisions
- Workplace accommodations
- Employee relations matters

## Access Requirements

> [!IMPORTANT]
> HR data is subject to privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.). Access violations may result in regulatory penalties.

### Eligibility Criteria

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Department | Human Resources, Legal |
| Training | HR Data Privacy (annual HRIS cert) |
| Approval | CHRO or HR Director |
| System Access | HRIS application credentials |

### Manager Access

Managers have limited access to:
- Direct reports' basic personnel info
- Performance review history (own team)
- Attendance records (own team)

Managers do NOT have access to:
- Compensation details of peers
- Disciplinary history of other teams
- Medical or accommodation information
- Investigation materials

### Data Subject Rights

Employees may request:
1. Access to their personal data
2. Correction of inaccurate data
3. Deletion (where legally permitted)
4. Portability of their records

Process requests through HR within 30 days.

## Special Categories

### Medical Information
- Stored separately from personnel file
- Accessible only to designated HR staff
- ADA accommodation details are need-to-know

### Investigation Records
- Access limited to investigating parties
- Legal hold applies during active matters
- Retention per legal guidance

### Whistleblower Protection
- Reporter identity is strictly confidential
- Separate access controls apply
- Retaliation is prohibited
