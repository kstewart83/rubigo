# Grape Compartment (ðŸ‡) - Executive & Board

## Overview

The Grape compartment protects **executive and board-level** communications including strategic decisions, board meeting materials, executive planning, and governance matters.

## Scope

This compartment covers:
- Board of Directors communications
- Executive Committee discussions
- Strategic planning documents
- CEO/CFO/CLO privileged communications
- Succession planning
- Executive compensation decisions
- Shareholder relations strategy

## Access Requirements

> [!CAUTION]
> Grape compartment access is highly restricted. Unauthorized access attempts trigger immediate security review.

### Eligibility

| Role | Access Level |
|------|--------------|
| Board Members | Full board materials |
| CEO | Full compartment |
| C-Suite Executives | Role-relevant sections |
| Executive Assistants | Limited, supervised access |
| Board Secretary | Administrative access |
| External Board Counsel | As-needed, logged |

### No Access by Default
- Vice Presidents (unless C-level or Board agenda item)
- Legal (except CLO and designated counsel)
- Finance (except CFO)
- IT Administrators (encryption-at-rest only)

## Board Materials

### Meeting Preparation
- Distribute via secure board portal only
- No email attachments for board decks
- 48-hour advance distribution minimum
- Version control mandatory

### During Meetings
- No personal devices in board sessions
- Notes taken by Secretary only
- Recording prohibited
- Materials collected at conclusion

### Post-Meeting
- Minutes approved before distribution
- Action items via secure portal
- Draft materials destroyed

## Executive Communications

### Secure Channels
- Use executive-only communication tools
- Encrypted messaging for sensitive topics
- No discussion on personal devices
- Travel precautions for international trips

### Delegation
- Executive assistants may access scheduling
- Content access requires explicit authorization
- Forwarding without approval prohibited

## Governance Requirements

### Audit Committee
- Separate sub-compartment
- External auditor communications
- Whistleblower escalations

### Compensation Committee
- Executive pay discussions
- Equity grant approvals
- Peer benchmarking data
